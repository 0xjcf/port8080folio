ABOUT ME

I'm Jose, a hands-on technical advisor who believes good systems start with clear thinking and honest tradeoffs. My approach is simple: reduce complexity, choose the smallest tool that works, and design around real constraints. I avoid buzzwords, explain decisions in plain English, and focus on changes that measurably improve delivery speed and quality.

I help founders and executives turn strategy into shipping. That means aligning product goals with architecture, introducing lightweight processes, and mentoring teams to operate with confidence.
